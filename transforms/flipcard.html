Directions

Welcome to the Lottery game! For this game you the player will need to guess 3 numbers all of them being between 1 and 10. Once you think of your 3 numbers press the cards below, if all 3 of your guesses are the correct numbers and in the correct order/card postions you win $1000. If only two of your guesses are correct and in the correct order/card postions you win $100. If only one of your guesses is correct and in the correct order/card postion than you win $10. Take your chances and play the game! Also, Have fun.
