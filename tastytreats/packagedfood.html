Favorite Packaged food

Kraft Mac and Cheese

My number one favorite packaged food is Kraft mac and cheese. I like this Mac and Cheese not because of the taste, even though it tastes amazing. I love it because it takes me back to my childhood. I rember growing up and playing in the snow for hours! Then I would come in and my mom would have a fresh pot of Kraft Mac and Cheese wating for me. As an Adult in my 20's when every I see the box I get the biggest smile on my face.

Ducan Hines Brownie Mix

This is a very close secound favorite for me. I like this brownie mix so much because it's the first thing I ever learned how to bake. I was around 9 years old. Even though making this box mix is very easy, it was a challenge for because grwoing up I always had trouble reading and following dicrections. Once I made these Brownies successfully it gave me hop that I could learn and complete school. To this day I am still working towards that goal.
